About

This webpage helps students explore the best career options available after completing 10th and 2nd PUC.

After 10th

  • Science Stream: For careers in Engineering, Medicine, Research.
  • Commerce Stream: For careers in Business, Finance, Accounting.
  • Arts / Humanities: For careers in Journalism, Law, Design, Civil Services.
  • Diploma Courses: Technical education in fields like Computer, Mechanical, Civil, etc.

After 2nd PUC

  • B.Sc, B.Com, B.A: Degree programs in various specializations.
  • Engineering (B.E/B.Tech): For Science students.
  • MBBS / Nursing / Pharmacy: For students interested in Medicine.
  • Professional Courses: CA, CS, BBA, Law, Design, etc.
